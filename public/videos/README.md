# Video Assets for iEmbrace Meditation App

## Video Requirements

Place your meditation videos in this folder with the following naming convention:

### Primary Videos:
- `meditation-1.mp4` - Main meditation/breathing video
- `meditation-2.mp4` - Secondary meditation video
- `meditation-3.mp4` (optional) - Third video if you have one

### Video Specifications:
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher
- **Duration**: 30 seconds to 2 minutes (will loop automatically)
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (under 50MB per video)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 landscape

### Poster Images (optional):
Place corresponding poster images in `/public/images/posters/`:
- `meditation-1-poster.jpg`
- `meditation-2-poster.jpg`
- `meditation-3-poster.jpg`

## What to Upload:

1. **Meditation Video 1**: Your first meditation/wellness video
2. **Meditation Video 2**: Your second meditation/wellness video
3. **Poster Images** (optional): Screenshot from each video to show while loading

## After Upload:

The videos will automatically be used in the hero section with:
- Smooth auto-switching every 8-10 seconds
- Smart preloading based on device capabilities
- Seamless loop playback
- Mobile optimization

Just drag and drop your video files into this folder!
