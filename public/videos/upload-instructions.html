🎬 Upload Your Meditation Videos

📁 Upload Instructions

To see your custom meditation videos as the hero background:

  1. Add your MP4 video files to /public/videos/
  2. Name them: meditation-1.mp4, meditation-2.mp4, meditation-3.mp4
  3. Refresh your browser to see the new backgrounds

📋 Video Requirements

  • Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
  • Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum (4K preferred)
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9
  • Duration: 10-60 seconds (will loop)
  • File Size: Under 10MB for web performance
  • Content: Meditation, nature, or calming visuals

🎨 Current Background

Until you upload videos, the hero section will display a beautiful gradient background.
